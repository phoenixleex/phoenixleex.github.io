About Me

I am an undergraduate student of Singapore Management University (SMU) pursuing a Bachelor’s degree of science with double major in Information Systems Management and Analytics.

Since my youth, I have a keen interest in puzzle solving and maze games, and through university education, a passion and knowledge in data analytics using technology.

In my university study, I was lured to new challenges and knowledge that demands critical thinking and analytical skills as well as exploiting existing data to solve real world problems. Through courses and projects I have gained hands on experience in Computational Statistics, Data Mining and Warehousing, visualization, security as well as a comprehensive understanding of business management and its alignment with IT

Before joining SMU, I have completed a polytechnic diploma in engineering. The diploma courses introduced me to the foundation of electronic principles, data communication protocols and security theories. All these major studies and projects experience can achieve a synergy that make me unique and help me excel in the job.

Besides the in-class studies, I am actively involved in various activities to broaden my knowledge and enhance my experiences in different disciplines.
